01

WELCOMEHow to play

  • 1. The homepage greets them with a menu and short description of what the challenges do
  • 2. On pressing the “get started” button, players will be taken directly to the available hunt of the day. Here they will be shown the objective and time available to complete
  • 3. The clock starts ticking and the first level is loaded. The player is presented with a question and 3 multiple choice answers. Depending on the answer, the user will be on the correct path or a detour. Every choice has weighted points. Remember it’s all about street smarts!
  • 4. If on correct path you will finish the hunt. We’ll show you points, and redeem prize. To redeem the points we’d need to capture your personal details. If on detour path, most likely time will run out and you’d need to start hunt again.
